Purworejo

Purworejo is a regency in the southern part of Central Java province in Indonesia

People usually stop by Purworejo to go to Yogyakarta

Although the tourism isn't that popular,

Purworejo still have several place you can visit on your holiday

Purworejo's typical food consists of: Dawet Ireng, Clorot, Geblek, etc.
